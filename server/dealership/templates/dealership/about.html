About Us

Our Mission

At Car Dealership Reviews, our mission is to provide consumers with honest, transparent information about car dealerships across the United States. We believe that informed consumers make better decisions, and we're committed to creating a platform where people can share their experiences and help others in their car-buying journey.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Car Dealership Reviews was born out of a frustrating car-buying experience. Our founder realized that there was no centralized platform for consumers to share their dealership experiences, making it difficult for others to know which dealerships provided excellent service and which ones to avoid.

What started as a simple idea has grown into a comprehensive platform that helps thousands of consumers make informed decisions about where to purchase their next vehicle.

How It Works

Our platform is simple to use:

  1. Browse dealerships by location or search for a specific dealership
  2. Read reviews from real customers who have interacted with the dealership
  3. Create an account to share your own experience and help others
  4. Our sentiment analysis technology helps categorize reviews as positive, negative, or neutral

Our Team

We're a small but dedicated team of automotive enthusiasts, consumer advocates, and technology experts. We're passionate about improving the car-buying experience for everyone and committed to maintaining a platform that's honest, transparent, and useful.
